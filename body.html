The Daily Life Of The Immortal King is a Chinese web novel written by Kuxuan. As of now, more than 1170 chapters have been translated into English, and as the story is still ongoing, you can expect more chapters soon.

This web novel has as protagonist Wang Ling, who is an exceptional cultivator who achieves a new level as the years go by.

As of now, he has become practically an "invincible being," and his powers are out of control. However, there are still challenges for him to face now that he has turned sixteen, including Senior High School. Even though he was looking forward to having a peaceful and quiet high school, his destiny seems to have taken the opposite path.

"The Daily Life Of The Immortal King" is a romantic comedy with a carefree protagonist that pretty much goes around doing whatever he wants. At the same time, he tries to hide his real power simultaneously. This leads to the funniest situations you could ever think of.

Although the protagonist tends to be a solitaire man, he makes some friends through the chapters and gets a love interest before he even realizes. Although the protagonist is powerful right from the start, other aspects of the novel are developed slowly. You wouldn't want a story with an overpowered protagonist to end up so quickly!

Although this novel is still ongoing, it sometimes goes through a hiatus, so there is a high possibility that after a while, you may have to wait for new episodes. However, you can be sure you'll have a great time while reading it! It's highly recommended if you want to read a parody of what you're used to seeing in cultivation or Xianxia novels.

Other elements that you could add into this mix are Slice of Life and Adventure, but you'll get to like the story after reading a couple of chapters.
